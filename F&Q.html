FAQ - Credit Hour System

What is the Credit Hour System?

The Credit Hour System is a method of measuring academic work, where each course is assigned a certain number of credit hours based on the number of hours spent in class per week.

How do I calculate my GPA?

Your GPA is calculated by dividing the total grade points earned by the total credit hours attempted. The grade points are determined based on your grades in each course.

How can I register for courses?

You can register for courses through the Student Records System (SRS) available on the CHS website. Ensure you meet the prerequisites for the courses you wish to take.

What happens if i miss the tuition payment deadline?

The tuition amount will increase by 10% as a late penalty.

Where can I find academic resources like study materials?

Academic resources can be accessed throug Thinqi or University's library. Check with the FEPS library for physical or digital resources related to your courses.
